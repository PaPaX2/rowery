Rowery górskie

Mountain bikes, or mtb bikes, are very durable. They are very useful for getting out of the pavement and crossing the pits in urban traffic.

Rowery crossowe

Tour bicycles are bicycles based on long distances and comfort with special designs with squad angles between road bikes and mountain bikes.

Rowery miejskie

City bikes are used for transportation in the city. Its position is upright so that you can easily control the traffic. It is suitable for carrying loads.

Rowery szosowe

Road bikes are special bicycle models that you can go to very high speeds due to gear ratios, geometry and wheel thicknesses.

Gravele

They are performance-oriented bicycles whose geometry and gear ratios are similar to road bikes, plus that they also allow driving on light terrain and gravel roads.

Rowery składane

Folding bikes fold and take a compact form when transported and stored. Thus, they are easy to carry and do not take up much space.

Fat Bike

They are bicycle models that can travel without burial in snow and sand with their wide tires far above the standards.

Rowery dziecięce

Bicycle models that have geometries according to child sizes in various age groups and attract children with their colorful designs.
